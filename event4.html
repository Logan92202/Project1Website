About Run Club

Join our weekly Run Club to stay fit, meet new friends, and explore scenic routes around the city! Whether you're a seasoned runner or just getting started, everyone is welcome to lace up and hit the pavement with us.

Date & Time

Date: Every Saturday
Time: 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Meeting Location

Bayfront Park, 301 Biscayne Blvd, Miami, FL 33132

Event Highlights

  • Guided warm-up and cool-down sessions
  • Flexible pace groups for all skill levels
  • Hydration stations along the route
  • Post-run coffee meetup
  • Exclusive discounts at partner sports stores

Membership

Membership is free! Sign up to receive route updates and event reminders.
