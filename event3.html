About Beach Night

Escape the ordinary and join us for a magical Beach Night event! Unwind with the sound of the waves, dance barefoot on the sand, and enjoy an evening of great music, delicious food, and unforgettable memories.

Date & Time

Date: Friday, December 15th, 2024
Time: 6:00 PM - 11:30 PM

Location

South Beach, 1200 Ocean Drive, Miami Beach, FL 33139

Event Highlights

  • Bonfire and s'mores station
  • Live acoustic performances and DJ sets
  • Food trucks with local cuisine
  • Interactive games and activities
  • Glow-in-the-dark beach accessories

Tickets

Tickets are $30 per person. Early bird discounts available until December 1st!
